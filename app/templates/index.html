Our Purpose


BookCamel was made by college students, for college students.

The seven creators of this site are all university students that were frustrated with the limited options for buying and selling textbooks.

We created this platform to help students like ourselves to afford their textbooks and make it easy to sell them when their courses are finished.

Here's to no more ridiculous buy-back offers from bookstores.
